What We're Aiming For...

Goal 1

Customizable Health Monitoring

Manual trackers lack real-time monitoring. By offering customizable health tracking, we address this gap, allowing pet owners to get timely updates tailored to specific needs.

Goal 2

Interactive Pet Communicatione

We want to build something that not only entertains but also enables meaningful interaction.

Goal 3

Fun Interface

A fun and engaging interface encourages pet owners to use the app consistently, making both health monitoring and communication more enjoyable. .

Insight #1

Many users, particularly those with aging or special-needs pets, expressed frustration with the lack of real-time health monitoring in current solutions.

Insight #2

People expressed the need for a personalized experience, as each pet‚Äôs behavior and health are unique.







Persona & Journey Map

After gathering insights from our user research, with guidance from our teacher, I created personas and a user journey map to focus our design on real user needs.

The personas, Shelly and Michael, represent two key user types and helped us better understand their frustrations and goals. The journey map for Shelly highlighted critical pain points, like setup challenges and navigation issues, guiding our design improvements. This collaborative approach ensured our design stayed user-centered.

  • Task 1: Login and create a pet profile.
  • Task 2: Check water intake.

Heuristic Evaluation

We then conducted a heuristic evaluation with three users on our high-fidelity prototype. They completed five tasks, providing valuable insights:

Final Outcome

Onboarding

Commitment doodle: Users begin with a personal doodle, symbolizing their dedication to their pet's well-being. The doodle reappears in the app, reminding owners of their commitment to their pet's health and happiness.

Health Log

Pet‚Äôs parents can simply glide through different health metrics‚Äîwater intake, heart rate, and sleep duration‚Äîby either swiping left or right or tapping to select. By focusing on functionality and convenience, it simplifies the monitoring process, making pet care management both intuitive and accessible.

Communication

Connect with your furry friends' voices and emotions effortlessly. Listen to daily barks or meows on the speech diary. Get insights into their mood with the mood report. Understand and bond with your pet like never before!

Fun Profile Page

Drawing inspiration from a cat's climbing tree, this page offers a playful way to explore pet data. Users can also drag interactive blocks.







Takeaways

Empathy Drives Innovation

Through this project, I discovered how empathy can drive design, enabling better communication between pets and their owners. By understanding pets as emotional beings, I realized that empathy can lead to innovative solutions, turning Purrpath into more than just a management tool‚Äîit's a way to deepen the connection between pets and their owners.

Balancing Theory and Practice

Creating a hypothetical app like Purrpath was a chance to blend creativity with practicality. It highlighted how theoretical ideas can be translated into real-world applications and the challenges of bringing a digital product to life.

Looking Ahead

Purrpath has shown me how technology can reshape pet care, enhancing the way we interact with our pets. It inspired me to think beyond current tech and explore future possibilities where design and technology create more meaningful experiences for pets and their owners.
