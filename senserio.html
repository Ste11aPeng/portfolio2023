Senserio

Context:

Exited wearables like rings and watches are often cold and hard in the chosen material. There is an emotional gap when we seek comfort and relief from stress interacting with these werables. I want to bridge the gap in current situations, so I made this hypothetic wearable, called Sensorio, which combines warmth and support to comfort people.

Role:

Individual Project

Time:

Winter 2024

Media:

Blender

Research

Primary Data

I recorded the moments of my personal hand behaviors when I feel anxious, such as fidgeting with rings and jewelry or rubbing fingers together. However, the inherent coldness and hardness of metal rings often fail to provide the desired comfort, my hands were still "deformed" by these behaviors.

After above observation, I also recorded the movement and position of myself being comforted and solaced, such as feel sunshine, be cuddked and holding a friend's hand. These actions serve as powerful sources of emotional reassurance, offering a tangible connection that addresses anxiety in some way. By understanding and replicating these comforting interactions, I wonder if other have the same situations. The truth is they do!

Secondary Data

Chart on the left is my interview note.I organized a focus group interview to delve into subconscious behaviors linked to negative emotions. The aim was to uncover subtle non-verbal cues exhibited by participants, shedding light on how emotions manifest involuntarily.

Over 80% of respondents often resort to subconscious hand movements in the moments of nervousness or anxiety, such as clenching fists or grasping objects, as a means of seeking comfort and relief.. Just like Me!

Ideation

Moodboard & Mindmap:

Here is my collage also my visual narrative to show my inspirations from previous research. Some components like nicotine is a typical anxiety coping mechanism; fire and warmth symbolize emotional support; and gloves are for confort and security; bubble wrap in the back is a tactile tool for stress relief, in the importance of physical sensations. Together, address the plausibility of the handcrafted wearable which aims to provide holistic relief for individuals navigating moments of discomfort.
