Context:

Apple Notes has the biggest advantage among all its competitors, the advantage of the ecosystem, offering a high accessibility and seamless integration with other Apple products.

The goal of this redesign is to enhance the voice recording functionalities, streamline note management processes, improve note interconnectivity, and incorporate features that cater to visual learners.
