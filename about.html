Info

My name "Stella" means "star" in Latin, while "粲" in my Chinese name also conveys the idea of brightness and brilliance. And just like a star, I believe art and design have the infinite power to illuminate and captivate.


During my freshman year of undergraduate, I studied industrial design, immersing myself in the fusion of productivity and functionality. However, it was during this time that I found myself drawn to a deeper passion – the dynamic interplay between human, technology, and their environment.


Driven by this newfound curiosity, I set out to delve into the realms of interaction design. Against the backdrop of Michigan's chilly landscapes, I found warmth in the human-centric approach to art and design, where every interaction tells a story and every experience leaves a lasting impression.

Edu

Penny Stamps, School of Art&Design & UMSI, School of Information, University of Michigan.

At U of M, I found both an exceptional educational platform and a community of inspiring peers who pushed me to grow. Collaborative projects taught me the transformative power of teamwork and mentorship, shaping me into the designer I am today.
